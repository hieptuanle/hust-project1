Data Deletion Instructions

How to Delete Your Data

We respect your privacy and make it easy to delete your data from our service. Here are the steps to delete your data based on the platform you're using:

Telegram Users

To delete your data from Telegram:

  1. Open your chat with Cake AI Bot
  2. Send the command: /delete_my_data
  3. Confirm your choice when prompted

Note: This will permanently delete all your conversation history and preferences.

Meta Platform Users

To delete your data from Meta platforms (Facebook Messenger):

  1. Open your conversation with Cake AI
  2. Type "delete my data"
  3. Follow the confirmation steps provided

Note: This will erase all your data including chat history and user preferences.

Additional Information

After requesting deletion, your data will be permanently removed within 14 days. For any questions or concerns, please contact our support team.

Please note that some information might be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes.
